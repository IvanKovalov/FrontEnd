About Us

Every vehicle Mazda builds is built around one key element – ​​you. Each car is designed not only to get you from point A to point B, but also to give you emotions. Give a sense of unity with your car and a sense of perfect harmony. Unity of rider and horse. This is Mazda's philosophy. According to Japanese tradition, we call this feeling Jinba Ittai. When you're behind the wheel of a Mazda, you'll never feel lonely. You feel a deep intuitive connection with the car. Every journey, every acceleration, braking or every turn is born in a harmony that cannot be described in words. We tried to make this feeling accompany you every time you drive a Mazda.Mazda has a fearless and indomitable spirit that gives it the courage to question conventional approaches and develop new solutions in engineering and design that others thought were impossible. Mazda also proved its technological excellence on the track, producing the first rotary-engined sports car and becoming the first Japanese brand to win the grueling 24 Hours of Le Mans. This is what drove the Mazda team from 1920 to the present day and continues to do so.
